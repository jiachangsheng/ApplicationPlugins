Description

The Microdesk Accelerator enhances MEP designers' modeling performance by automating simple, frequent steps in their everyday workflow.

As Revit continues to introduce new features and the need to get projects out the door faster increases, Microdesk has developed a suite of purpose-built applications to speed production of MEP models, automate low value tasks and free up your team’s time to focus on design and sustainability.

BIMrx builds on the Accelerator application commands, providing features that increase modeling efficiency, improve project management and streamline documentation.  

For more information on BIMrx, contact Microdesk support or visit our website:

https://www.microdesk.com/bimrx/

General Usage Instructions

Keep producing and coordinating while remaining in a 3D view

The Bloom feature starts the 3D drafting process. Simply run the command and click on any item with MEP connectors, and watch as duct and pipe extend from every connector automatically.

Next, our elbow commands allow designers to quickly draw bends while working in a 3D view.  Simply turn up, turn right and be on your way.  

The move-connect and move-connect-align commands align MEP families and make logical connections. Use it to connect branch ductwork to its new VAV box location, or to connect a sanitary line to a water closet or lavatory.  You choose whether the moving object should also be aligned to the connector when it is moved.  

Flip multiple solves the simple problem of flipping the work plane of multiple objects at once and works great with objects that are frequently hosted like outlets, lights, and air terminals.

Installation/Uninstallation

The installer that ran when you downloaded this app/plug-in from Autodesk App Store will start installing the app/plug-in. OR, simply double-click the downloaded installer to install the app/plugin.

You may need to restart the Autodesk product to activate the app/plug-in. You will be asked to enter an email address to register the product. This is a free registration. Note: if the tools aren't activated, try restarting Autodesk® Revit® again.

To uninstall this plug-in, exit the Autodesk product if you are currently running it, simply rerun the installer, and select the "Uninstall" button. OR, click Control Panel > Programs > Programs and Features (Windows 7/8.1/10/11) and uninstall as you would any other application from your system.
